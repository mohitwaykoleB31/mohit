About Us

Welcome to Killiney Kopitiam, a slice of Singapore's rich coffeehouse heritage, now in Berndorf, Germany. Established in Singapore in 1919, Killiney Kopitiam has been serving up traditional kopi (coffee) and iconic local breakfast delights for over a century. We’re proud to bring this time-honored tradition to Berndorf, offering a cozy, authentic atmosphere where guests can experience the flavors and warmth of Singapore’s beloved kopitiam culture. At Killiney Kopitiam, we specialize in freshly brewed coffee, traditional kaya toast, soft-boiled eggs, and other local favorites, all served with a side of friendly hospitality. Whether you’re here for a quiet moment, a coffee break, or a meal with friends, we provide the perfect place to relax, unwind, and indulge in Singapore's best-loved coffeehouse offerings. Join us at Killiney Kopitiam, where every cup tells a story and every bite takes you back to a tradition that spans generations.
